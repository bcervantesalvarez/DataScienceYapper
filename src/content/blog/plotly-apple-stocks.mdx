An interactive Plotly walkthrough of Apple's stock price history from 1981 to
the present. The narrative highlights the post-2010 acceleration driven by the
iPhone era and uses custom ggplot/Plotly theming to make the chart actually
worth reading.

Mostly a craft post — how to take a time-series visualization from "the
defaults" to something that looks like it belongs in a finished report.
