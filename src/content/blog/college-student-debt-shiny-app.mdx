A Shiny app built with Corey Cassell to help prospective college students reason
about the financial side of their major. It bundles four tools — salary estimator,
tuition estimator, debt estimator, and debt calculator — each tied to underlying
datasets on majors, tuition, and earnings.

The goal was a quick gut-check on the ROI side of a major choice without pretending
it could replace a financial advisor.
