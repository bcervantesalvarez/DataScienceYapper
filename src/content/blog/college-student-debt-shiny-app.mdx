<Overview>
  A dashboard built with Corey Cassell to help prospective college students reason about the financial side of their major choice. Four tools — salary, tuition, debt, and a debt calculator — each tied to underlying datasets on majors, tuition, and earnings.
</Overview>

## Notes from the archive

The goal was a quick gut-check on the ROI of a major choice, not a replacement for a financial advisor. Data behind each tool came from publicly available college-scorecard and BLS sources.
