A retrospective on building a Shiny dashboard over a Kaggle dataset of Amazon
India products. The app surfaces categories and subcategories with the highest
average ratings and the most reviews.

The post reflects on the workflow improvements — advanced CSS theming, faster
iteration cycles — compared to earlier Shiny projects.
