A Plotly-driven exploration of a large gym dataset, ranking workout types by
user ratings. Stretching scores lowest for muscle building — which lines up with
the broader literature suggesting resistance training should be the primary
driver of hypertrophy, with stretching reserved for flexibility and recovery.

Worth keeping mostly as a Plotly-on-a-real-dataset example, plus a memorable
headline finding.
