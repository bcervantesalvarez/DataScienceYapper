## Context

Co-authored with Willa Van Liew and Hans Lehndorff. Cox proportional-hazards models test whether higher temperatures meaningfully shorten survival times, with attention to interaction effects between exposure level and ambient conditions. The applied point: as freshwater systems warm under climate pressure, doses that were sub-lethal start producing measurable mortality, and survival analysis is the natural tool for quantifying that shift.
