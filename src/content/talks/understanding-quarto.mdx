An introduction to Quarto as the modern successor to RMarkdown for reproducible
writing, slides, and websites. The talk walks through what makes Quarto a real
upgrade — multi-language execution, formal project structure, better defaults —
and where it fits into a data scientist's publishing workflow.

By the end of the session, attendees can stand up a Quarto project, render it to
HTML and PDF, and understand the trade-offs versus sticking with RMarkdown.
