## Context

Income, home ownership, and loan amount are the headline predictors. The unexpected finding — credit score's coefficient runs counter to intuition in the fitted model — becomes the pedagogical anchor for a discussion of specification error, omitted-variable bias, and why a counterintuitive sign is a prompt to inspect the model rather than ship it.
