## Context

Covers MCAR, MAR, and MNAR with concrete examples, then shows the failure modes of the naive options (listwise deletion, mean imputation, last-observation-carried-forward). The back half applies Multiple Imputation by Chained Equations via the `mice` package, with worked examples of convergence diagnostics, imputation-quality checks, and how to propagate uncertainty through to the final estimate.
