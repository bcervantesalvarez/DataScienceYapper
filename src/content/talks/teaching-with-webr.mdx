A live talk on integrating webR into existing R coursework. Where the blog post
makes the case in writing, this session demonstrates the workflow in real time —
embedding live executable R blocks in Quarto slides, building reactive lab exercises,
and removing the install-the-tidyverse opening lecture from the syllabus entirely.

The pitch: instructors get to spend their time on content, and students get
immediate feedback instead of fighting their environment.
