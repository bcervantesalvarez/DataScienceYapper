## Context

Live demonstrations of embedding executable R chunks in Quarto slides and lab handouts, building reactive lab exercises, and removing the "install the tidyverse" opening lecture from the syllabus entirely. The pitch lands fast: instructors spend their time on content, students get instant feedback, and the cross-platform headache disappears.
