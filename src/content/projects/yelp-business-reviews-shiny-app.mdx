A Shiny dashboard that pairs Yelp business reviews with geostatistical modeling.
Users can filter businesses on an interactive map, compare star ratings against
features like credit-card acceptance or operating hours, and tune variogram
parameters — sill, range, nugget, kappa — to explore the spatial autocorrelation
of ratings.

Built as a teaching tool as much as an explorer: the controls intentionally
expose the geostatistical knobs so students can feel how each parameter changes
the kriged surface.
