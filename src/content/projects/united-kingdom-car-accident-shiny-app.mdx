An interactive Shiny dashboard for exploring historical car-accident data across the
United Kingdom. Users can filter by region and time window, slice trends across years
and months, and download the underlying dataset for their own analyses.

The app pairs `tidyverse` data shaping with `shiny` reactivity and `plotly` for the
charts, with sensible defaults so a first-time visitor sees the headline trend before
they touch a single control.

[**Open the live app ↗**](https://bcervantesalvarez.shinyapps.io/UKAccidentTimeSeries/)
