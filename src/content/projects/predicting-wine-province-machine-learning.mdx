An NLP-flavored Random Forest model that predicts the province of origin for
Pinot Noir wines from critic tasting notes. Text is tokenized, stemmed, and
pivoted into a sparse feature matrix; the resulting classifier achieves a Kappa
of 0.82.

The use case is a sommelier-style tool: drop in a few sentences of tasting copy
and get back a probability over likely origins.
