<Overview>
  A text classifier that reads tasting notes for Pinot Noir and predicts the wine's province of origin. The use case is a sommelier-style tool: drop in a few sentences of tasting copy, get back a probability over likely origins.
</Overview>

## Notes from the archive

Tokenization, stemming, and a pivot into a sparse feature matrix on the front end; a Random Forest classifier on the back end. Reached kappa 0.82 on held-out data. The interesting finding was not the score but which descriptors (specific fruits, oak notes) carried the most predictive weight, hinting at consistent regional vocabulary in the critic corpus.
