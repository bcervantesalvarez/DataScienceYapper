A machine-learning project forecasting whether customers will return a
product. The headline result: AUC moved from a 0.625 baseline to 0.98 (97.8%
accuracy, Kappa 0.95) after layering regional demographics, customer age, price
range, seasonality, and population-standardized return rates onto the feature set.

The lesson is the usual one — most of the lift came from the feature engineering,
not the choice of estimator.
