A longitudinal report dissecting U.S. healthcare expenditure from 1980 through
2014 across categories, regions, and states. After reshaping a wide Kaggle dataset
into tidy form, the analysis visualizes the consistent upward trend and discusses
contributing factors — population growth, wage inflation, the shift toward chronic
care.

Built as a teaching artifact: the data wrangling and visualization steps are
written out at length so the methodology is reproducible.
