A Quarto dashboard built for Posit::conf 2024 that visualizes Olympic 100m
results alongside broader medal data. The point of the piece: Quarto's dashboard
format on its own — no Shiny server, no runtime compute — is enough to deliver
polished, multi-panel analytics built on `plotly` and `gt`.

Useful as a reference for anyone who wants the look and feel of a dashboard
without the deployment overhead of a reactive backend.
